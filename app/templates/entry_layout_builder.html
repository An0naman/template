How to Use
  • Edit Mode: Click "Edit Layout" to enable dragging and resizing
  • Add Sections: Click sections in the palette to add them to the layout
  • Rearrange: Drag sections to reposition them
  • Resize: Drag section corners to resize
  • Configure: Click a section to edit its properties
  • Hide/Show: Use the eye icon to toggle section visibility
  • Save: Click "Save Layout" to apply changes
